COMMUNITY ORGANISATION

Community Contract

To create a Thriving Temple Quarter Community, I agree to meet these social commitments:

1

Support the TQ Community

Support thriving key themes: Social (Building capital), Environmental (Sustainability), Economic (Skills & training), and Wellbeing (Health).

Timeframe: Immediately on signing
2

Employ Locally Where Possible

Target a minimum of 50% of roles filled by Temple Quarter residents.

Timeframe: 18 Months
3

Pay a Real Living Wage

Pay at or above the Real Living Wage for all staff, including contracted and agency workers.

Timeframe: Immediately on signing / Annual Adjustments
4

Pursue a Diverse & Inclusive Workforce

Actively recruit from underrepresented groups and publish an annual EDI plan with measurable targets.

Timeframe: First plan within 12 months
