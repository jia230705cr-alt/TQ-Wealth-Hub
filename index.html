Our Vision

🌿

TQ Community Hive is built on the principles of a wellbeing economy — one that measures success not just in profit, but in the health, connection, and resilience of the whole community.

🤝

We believe that when businesses and organisations are rooted in a place, accountable to it, and actively reinvesting in it, they become the building blocks of a genuinely thriving neighbourhood.

The Hive brings together generative organisations — businesses and community groups that create and circulate value locally rather than extracting it — to form a living ecosystem in Temple Quarter. Through shared funding, mutual support, and collective action, members help grow a local economy that works for everyone.

Who Can Join Us?

Generative Businesses

Locally rooted businesses that distribute value back into the community rather than extracting it.

  • Be owned by a TQ resident or rooted locally
  • Have a generative structure (Worker-owned, Co-op, etc.)
  • Employ locally (>50% TQ residents)
  • Spend locally & prioritise local supply chains
  • Pay a Real Living Wage
  • Pursue a diverse and inclusive workforce
  • Reinvest in TQ where possible

Community Organisations

VCSE organisations that build social capital and wellbeing — the glue of our neighbourhood.

  • Be locally owned and run
  • Aim to support the TQ community
  • Social - Building inclusive social capital
  • Environmental - Empowering sustainable living
  • Economic - Building skills & opportunity
  • Wellbeing - Supporting mental & physical health

Why Join the Hive?

For Businesses

Access Funding: Monthly grants — no repayment, no equity.

Local Trade Network: Partnerships and customer referrals across TQ.

Social Value: Unlock procurement opportunities & recognition.

For Community Orgs

Sustainable Income: Reliable reinvestment from Hive businesses.

Expert Support: Mentoring from leaders, academics and experts.

Amplified Voice: Vote on funding and shape Hive priorities.
