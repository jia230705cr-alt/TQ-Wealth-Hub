Our Vision

Wellbeing First

Rooted Economy

How the Hive Works

STEP 01

JOIN

Free membership via a simple community promise. We support your transition to generative.

STEP 02

CONNECT

Access the Hub to trade, partner, and gain support from community and business experts.

STEP 03

APPLY

Monthly themed funding rounds. A panel shortlists, then the community votes on the winners.

STEP 04

REINVEST

Surplus is reinvested to fund vital community orgs, building wealth that stays in Temple Quarter.
