Our Vision

🧘‍♀️

TQ Community Hive is built on the principles of a wellbeing economy — one that measures success not just in profit, but in the health, connection, and resilience of the whole community.

🏘️

We believe that when businesses and organisations are rooted in a place, accountable to it, and actively reinvesting in it, they become the building blocks of a thriving neighbourhood.

"The Hive brings together generative organisations to form a living ecosystem... helping grow a local economy that works for everyone."

Who Can Join Us?

Generative Businesses

Locally rooted businesses that distribute value back into the community rather than extracting it.

  • Be owned by a TQ resident or rooted locally
  • Have a generative structure (Worker-owned, Co-op, etc.)
  • Employ locally (>50% TQ residents)
  • Spend locally & prioritise local supply chains
  • Pay a Real Living Wage
  • Reinvest in TQ where possible
Register as Business

Community Organisations

VCSE organisations that build social capital and wellbeing — the glue of our neighbourhood.

  • Be locally owned and run
  • Aim to support the TQ community
  • Social - Building inclusive social capital
  • Environmental - Empowering sustainable living
  • Economic - Building skills & opportunity
  • Wellbeing - Supporting mental & physical health

Why Join the Hive?

Free membership. Real funding. A network that works for you.

For Businesses

For Community Orgs

Who Runs the Hive?

Bristol BID

Founded by BBID to kickstart the ecosystem. They provide initial funding and hold a seat on the board to ensure continuity.

The Board

Elected members and community leaders (mostly TQ residents). They are paid for their time and decide funding focus each year.

The Members

The core of the Hive. Members vote on funding decisions, suggest where money is needed, and can be elected to the board.

Reminder: Membership is always free. The commitment is to the community promise.
