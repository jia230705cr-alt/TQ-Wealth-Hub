TQ Wealth Hive

Community Contract

For Generative Businesses in Temple Quarter

To create a thriving Temple Quarter, I agree to meet these social commitments with the support of the Hive:

  • 1

    Convert to a Generative Structure

    Transition to a worker-owned, co-operative, or wealth-sharing model that distributes value among owners, workers and consumers.

    Timeframe: Review within 1 year - Convert within 10 Years
  • 2

    Employ Locally Where Possible

    Target a minimum of 50% of roles filled by Temple Quarter residents.

    Timeframe: 18 Months
  • 3

    Spend Locally Where Possible

    Actively prioritise local supply chains for procurement of goods and services. Map current spend within 6 months and set annual improvement targets.

    Timeframe: Map in 6 months. Annual targets set from Year 1.
  • 4

    Pay a Real Living Wage

    Pay at or above the Real Living Wage (as set by the Living Wage Foundation) for all staff, including contracted and agency workers.

    Timeframe: Immediately on signing. Adjust annually.
  • 5

    Pursue a Diverse & Inclusive Workforce

    Actively recruit from underrepresented groups. Publish an annual Equality, Diversity & Inclusion plan with measurable targets and honest reporting.

    Timeframe: First plan within 12 months. Annual public report thereafter.
