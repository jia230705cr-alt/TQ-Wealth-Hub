Wealth Hive Funding

We provide regenerative capital to businesses that go above and beyond for Temple Quarter. To be eligible for our funding rounds, you must meet the following criteria:

  • 75%+ Local Employment (Staff living in TQ postcodes)
  • Wealth Distribution (Commitment to fair pay gap)
  • Local Spend First (Spending within the TQ ecosystem)
  • Profit Sharing (Reinvesting back into the community fund)
  • Generative Operations (Prioritizing social/eco value)
  • Worker Empowerment (Involving staff in decisions)

* Qualifying businesses can apply for grants and zero-interest loans ranging from £5,000 to £50,000.
